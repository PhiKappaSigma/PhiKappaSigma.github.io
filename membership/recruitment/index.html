RECRUITMENT

Skullhouse is keen to welcome into membership all men of good character who share a common interest in our core values, so that we can work together to further our objectives and to provide mutual support to each other as members of the brotherhood.

If you have read the FAQs and Membership Expectations and express an interest in becoming a Skull, please fill out this form.

Application to the Fraternity is open to all men of good character, but please be aware that all applications are subject to several stages of review by the full brotherhood at New York University.

Not all applications are successful.
